// 02 // ECONOMY

LEVELING GUIDE

4

LEVEL 4 — CONDITIONAL

Level up if you have upgrades available + a good item component. Don't force it—eco first.

5

LEVEL 5 — ALMOST ALWAYS

Level to 5 always unless you're on a 3× loss streak and looking to eco hard.

6

LEVEL 6 — WINSTREAK / RICH

Level on 4+ winstreak OR if you can maintain above 40g after leveling.

7

LEVEL 7 — ECONOMY CHECK

Level if you can stay above 30g after the investment. Preserve interest thresholds.

8

LEVEL 8 — KEY POWER SPIKE

Roll aggressively for Kai'Sa 2★ and Rift Herald 2★. Push to Level 9 after hitting.

⚠ DO NOT go below 10g while rolling
9

LEVEL 9 — HP DEPENDENT

High HP → Wait until Stage 5-5  |  Medium HP → Roll 20g first  |  Low HP → Level ASAP to stabilize with Wukong → Volibear

10

LEVEL 10 — ENDGAME

Only when carry is fully itemized + upgraded. Slot in Baron Nashor. Transfer best items + best Void mutation.

// 03 // STRATEGY

STAGE BREAKDOWN

STAGE 2-3
Early Game
⚔
  • Slam items aggressively — don't save components
  • Play for winstreak to build gold interest buffer
  • Run Kog'Maw + Malzahar + Rek'Sai as core
  • Keep strongest available board — don't force economy at cost of HP
🌀 RIFT HERALD UNLOCK
  • Have Void active for 8 player combats
  • No level requirement — auto-unlocks after 8 Void combats
  • Tip: 2 Void units already activate the trait — get them on board ASAP to start the 8-combat counter
STAGE 4
Mid Game
🎯
  • Roll on Level 8 for key 2-stars
  • Priority: Kai'Sa 2★ first, then Rift Herald 2★
  • Push to Level 9 after stabilizing
  • Flex Swain or Shyvana if available
  • Never roll below 10g — interest matters
🔓 KAI'SA UNLOCK
  • Reach Level 7
  • Have a Longshot unit on board with 3 items equipped
  • Both conditions met → Kai'Sa unlocks automatically
⚡ VOLIBEAR UNLOCK
  • Reach Level 8
  • Have a unit that starts combat with at least 3800 HP
  • Tip: Rift Herald or Cho'Gath with tank items easily hit this threshold
STAGE 5+
Late Game
👑
  • Full 9 Void + Bruiser board active
  • Wukong → Volibear slot for Bruiser trait
  • Push Level 10 when carry is itemized
  • Slot in Baron Nashor as capstone unit
👑 BARON NASHOR UNLOCK
  • Reach Level 10
  • Field 7 unique Void units simultaneously
  • Baron counts as 2 team slots — plan your roster accordingly
  • Tip: Transfer your best items to Baron immediately after unlocking
